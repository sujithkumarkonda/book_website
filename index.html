Fiction

The White Tiger

by Aravind Adiga

A dark and humorous novel about class struggle in India and personal transformation.

The God of Small Things

by Arundhati Roy

A story of forbidden love and societal politics in a small Indian town.

The Shadow Lines

by Amitav Ghosh

A poignant exploration of memory, borders, and identity.

Malgudi Days

by R. K. Narayan

A collection of heartwarming stories set in the fictional town of Malgudi.

Self-Help & Lifestyle

The Immortals of Meluha

by Amish Tripathi

A mythological tale that imparts lessons on life, leadership, and inner strength.

The Secret of the Nagas

by Amish Tripathi

A continuation of the Shiva trilogy that explores the struggle between good and evil within oneself.

Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life

by Héctor García & Francesc Miralles

A guide to finding your "ikigai," the reason for being, and living a more purposeful life.

Life Lessons from Monks

by Gaur Gopal Das

Insights on how to live a balanced and meaningful life from a renowned monk.

Indian Classics

The Guide

by R. K. Narayan

An intriguing story of transformation from a tour guide to a spiritual guide.

Gitanjali

by Rabindranath Tagore

A collection of poems that reflect deep spirituality and a connection to the divine.

Mother

by Maxim Gorky

An inspiring novel that delves into a mother’s transformative journey through revolutionary ideals.

Choker Bali

by Rabindranath Tagore

A timeless Bengali classic about love, betrayal, and the complexities of human relationships.

Decision-Making & Personal Growth

Stay Hungry Stay Foolish

by Rashmi Bansal

A compilation of stories about entrepreneurs who took bold risks and changed their lives, offering insights into decision-making.

Think Like a Monk

by Jay Shetty

A guide to living a peaceful and purposeful life, drawing from ancient wisdom and practical tips.

The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari

by Robin Sharma

A motivational fable about achieving one’s dreams and living a balanced life.

Mindset: The New Psychology of Success

by Carol S. Dweck

Explores the power of mindset in shaping success and how decision-making can be improved with a growth mindset.

Psychology of the Mind, Peace & Self-Discipline

Atomic Habits

by James Clear (Indian Edition)

A comprehensive guide to forming good habits and breaking bad ones.

The Power of Your Subconscious Mind

by Joseph Murphy (Indian Edition)

A timeless classic that helps you unlock the power of the subconscious mind to achieve success and peace.

The Power of Now

by Eckhart Tolle (Indian Edition)

A spiritual guide to living in the present and achieving inner peace.

Think and Grow Rich

by Napoleon Hill (Indian Edition)

A personal development and self-improvement guide to help develop the mindset for success and discipline.
